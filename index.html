Because doodling is cool...

What is this 'doodle'?

According to wikipdia a doodle is: a drawing made while a person's attention is otherwise occupied. Doodles are simple drawings that can have concrete representational meaning or may just be abstract shapes.

So why should I doodle?

Doodling has many benefits, some of the more scientific are that doodling strengthens fine motor skills, it encourages learning, the recall of information and creative thinking. It also helps to build neural connections in your brain. But aside form all of that sience babble - its relaxing and great fun!

Its 100% free!

Yes - you read that right! Well, you'll need to procure some kind of implement to make marks onto some kind of surface but there are no subscriptions, no yearly plans, no special training courses needed, no data-band-width limits! Plus it can be done anywhere, any time, by anyone - now grab a pen and something to doodle on!

But to doodle I need to be an artist / creative right?

That's totally true... so good job you are then! That's right - you already are creative; all humanoids are born this way, you just may not of realised it yet. The power of the doodle resides inside us all, you just have to release it.

What can I do with my doodle

Whatever you like! Keep it, frame it, give it to somebody, have it analyzed by a clever person with a phd after their name to tell you something about yourself. Its yours. You did it!

Get involved!

Spread the doodle love and lets get the whole world doodling in one huge doodlathon! (that's like a marathon, or triatholon, but for doodlers)
