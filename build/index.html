A mission to doodle for the future of humanity

What is this 'doodlathon'?

It's like a marathon, or a triathlon, but for people who don't really dig the sweat and pain of such physical events and prefer to sit and, erm, doodle...

The world is fast going down the shitter, so I propose that we all doodle, in one large global collaborative doodlathon to bring a back a bit of community based creativity.

But first, what is a doodle, and why should i do one?

According to wikipdia a doodle is: a drawing made while a person's attention is otherwise occupied. Doodles are simple drawings that can have concrete representational meaning or may just be abstract shapes.

Doodling has many benefits, some of the more scientific are that doodling strengthens fine motor skills, it encourages learning, the recall of information and creative thinking. It also helps to build neural connections in your brain. But aside form all of that science babble - its relaxing and great fun!

Bonus points! Its 100% free!

Yes - you read that right! Well, you'll need to procure some kind of implement to make marks onto some kind of surface but there are no subscriptions, no yearly plans, no special training courses needed, no data-band-width limits! Plus it can be done anywhere, any time, by anyone - now grab a pen and something to doodle on!

But to doodle I need to be creative right?

That's totally true... so good job you are then! That's right - you already are creative; all humanoids are born this way, you just may not of realised it yet. The power of the doodle resides inside us all, you just have to release it.
